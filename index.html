Remote Sensing & Image Processing

Remote sensing is the acquisition of information about an object or phenomenon without making physical contact with the object and thus in contrast to on-site observation.
To acquiring information from remotely sensed images, image progressing plays a significant role .
Image processing is processing of images using mathematical operations by using any form of signal processing, and the result of this progress may be either an image or a set of characteristics or parameters related to the image.

Environment & Wetlands

Environment is rapidly changing due to various reasons such as urbanization. Actually, wetlands are able to perceive the changes sensitively.
A wetland is a land area that is inundated with water, either permanently or seasonally, such that it takes on the characteristics of a distinct ecosystem.
To know more about how environment changes and how human impact on it, we start from wetlands.

Geoscience & Geography

Geoscience is the study of our planet’s physical characteristics, from earthquakes to raindrops, and floods to fossils.
Geography is an all-encompassing discipline that seeks an understanding of the Earth and its human and natural complexities—not merely where objects are, but how they have changed and come to be.
